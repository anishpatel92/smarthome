Best Smart Switches Under ₹2,000 (India, 2025)

switchesbudgetindia
Prices vary by marketplace and city. Always check warranty and return policy.

What to look for

  • ISI or BIS compliance
  • Physical toggle override
  • 2.4 GHz Wi‑Fi, no hub
  • App stability and clear wiring labels

Picks

  • 1-gang basic Wi‑Fi switch — reliable app, works with Alexa/Assistant
  • 2-gang with energy stats — slightly higher price, clean faceplate
  • Retrofit module for existing plate — hides inside box, electrician recommended

Install tips

  • Turn off MCB before touching wires
  • Label line/neutral
  • Test each gang before closing the box
